The Master Emerald - is a massive, mysterious gem, held on Angel Island and guarded by Knuckles the Echidna. Noted to possess massive energy readings akin to a giant Chaos Emerald, the Master Emerald is one of the most powerful relics on earth. Rings - Sonic was given a bag of rings from Longclaw when he was being chased by the Echidna Tribe.
