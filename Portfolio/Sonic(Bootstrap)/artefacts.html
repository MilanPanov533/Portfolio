Artefacts

The Chaos Emeralds

Originally created over 8000 years ago. At the time, they had already discovered Chaos energy but were seeking ways to safely harness it and use it as a fuel source. Very dangerous in wrong hands, Sonic uses them to transform himself in Super Form, Eggman created Shadow the hedgehog using power of Chaos.

The Master Emerald

A massive, mysterious gem, held on Angel Island and guarded by Knuckles the Echidna. Noted to possess massive energy readings akin to a giant Chaos Emerald, the Master Emerald is one of the most powerful relics on earth. In fact, the special abilities it commands alone makes it very powerful.

Rings

Sonic was given a bag of rings from Longclaw when he was being chased by the Echidna Tribe. He later lost them in a confrontation with Tom Wachowski, requiring him to venture to San Francisco to retrieve them (first movie event). In first Sonic 16 bit games the rings act as Sonic's lifeline.

Cyberspace portals

Portals scattered throughout the Starfall Islands. Most of these levels have the design of Green Hill, Chemical Plant, and Sky Sanctuary Zones, but their layout is inspired by many other stages from the numerous past 3D Sonic games.
